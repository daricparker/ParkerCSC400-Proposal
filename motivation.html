Motivation

According to a study published in the journal Obesity in February 2021, using digital tools to track what you eat, when you exercise, and how much you weigh will help you achieve your weight loss goals. The research shows that any form of recording can help people lose weight, however digital resources such as applications and websites, often keep people engaged for longer periods of time, resulting in more weight loss. Using an application which helps the user keep track of their meals will not only allow the user to eat more healthy but also can change their spending habits in a positive way.

Staying healthy does not only include diet, which is why there is also exercise tracking. Together as a unit, it is the first practical step to a healthy lifestyle. Not only are these applications easy to use, but they are accessible, convenient and does not take much time or monetary commitment such as seeking nutritional advices from a dietician or a gym trainer. Fit-Forward aims to be a central hub for those who wants to keep track of both their diet and physical activity in form of exercises to help the user reach their health goals with low monetary effort on their end, as well as motivating them to put in the effort and time.
