Architecture

Fit-Forward will be a web-based application which will utilize the server/client architecture.

The database will include information such as users, their daily dietary intake, meals, and also their exercise logs, stats and routine.

The data on nutritional foods will be fetched through an API provided by FoodData Central which is part of the U.S. Department of Agriculture, Agricultural Research Service. For data on various exercises and the burning of calories, the tentative API will be provided by wger. For displaying data through charts to the users, we will be using Google Charts.
